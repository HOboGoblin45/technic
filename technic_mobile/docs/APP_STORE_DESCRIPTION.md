## Description (4000 characters max)

Technic is your intelligent trading companion, combining quantitative analysis with AI insights to help you discover high-potential stock setups.

Whether you're a swing trader looking for quality entries or an active investor seeking data-driven insights, Technic delivers professional-grade scanning tools in a clean, intuitive interface.

**SMART STOCK SCANNER**
Scan thousands of stocks using proven technical patterns and quantitative filters. Our scanner identifies setups based on:
- Technical strength and momentum
- Volume patterns and breakouts
- Sector rotation and relative strength
- Custom filter combinations

**AI COPILOT**
Chat with our AI assistant to understand market movements and get explanations of complex trading setups. Ask questions in plain English about:
- Why a stock is showing up in scans
- Technical pattern explanations
- Market sector analysis
- Risk assessment for positions

**REAL-TIME CHARTS**
Interactive price charts with essential technical indicators:
- Candlestick and line charts
- Support and resistance levels
- Volume analysis
- Momentum signals and trends

**PRICE ALERTS**
Set custom price targets and get notified instantly when stocks hit your levels:
- Price above/below thresholds
- Percentage change alerts
- Push notifications even when app is closed
- Manage all alerts in one place

**TRADING IDEAS**
Access curated daily trade ideas with complete analysis:
- Entry and exit targets
- Risk/reward ratios
- Technical reasoning
- Track and save to your watchlist

**PERSONALIZED WATCHLIST**
Build and organize your watchlist:
- Quick access to favorite stocks
- Real-time price updates
- One-tap to set alerts
- Seamless sync across devices

**WHO IS TECHNIC FOR?**
- Active traders seeking an edge in the market
- Swing traders looking for quality setups
- Investors wanting data-driven insights
- Anyone looking to level up their trading

**BUILT FOR TRADERS**
- Dark theme optimized for extended use
- Fast, responsive interface
- Minimal data usage
- Regular updates with new features

Download Technic and start trading smarter today.
